How we work
You describe your problem in a message to the phone, Facebook or email. Next, we will arrange a meeting and we will pick up your motorcycle at your address at a convenient time for you. Delivery within 20 miles from the Riverside for free (for works from $100 and up). Further, all the work is accompanied by detailed photos. That is, you are aware of everything we do with your motorcycle. Upon completion of work, we deliver the motorcycle to your address at a convenient time for you. Then you make the payment.
